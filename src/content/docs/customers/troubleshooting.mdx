This page is being built. While we fill it in, common issues:

## Can't log in

- **Forgot password** → use the "Reset password" link on the sign-in page
- **Sign-in link expired** → ask your operator to resend the invite
- **Google sign-in not working** → check that your Google account email matches the one your operator invited; if not, contact your operator

## Missing a production

- Productions appear in the Client Portal when an operator runs them for your account. If you expected one and don't see it, ask your operator to confirm it ran.
- If it's there but not approved/published, you may need to **approve** it via the [rights review screen](/customers/rights-review/).

## Subscription issue

- See [Billing](/customers/billing/) or [Contact support](/general/contact-support/).

For anything else, [contact support](/general/contact-support/).
