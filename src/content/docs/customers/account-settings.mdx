Your account settings control your profile, how JARAI reaches you, and your security.
Most of this lives under **Settings** in the Client Portal.

<VideoHelper src="customer-settings.mp4" title="Manage your account settings">

1. Open **Settings** in the Client Portal.
2. Update your **profile** (name, picture) and **contact** details.
3. Set **notification preferences** (what you're told about, and how).
4. Review **security** — your sign-in and connected identities.

</VideoHelper>

## Profile & contacts

Keep your **name**, **profile picture**, and **contact email** current — the contact
email is where review and billing notifications go, so an out-of-date address means
missed approvals.

## Notification preferences

Choose which [notifications](/customers/notifications/) you receive and on which
channels (in-app, email, push). Turn down noise you don't need, but keep
**approval-required** notifications on — those are how you know content is waiting.

## Security & sign-in

You sign in with your email (and, where configured, a federated identity like
Google). JARAI uses a hosted, standards-based sign-in:

- If you can't sign in, use the **password reset / magic link** flow on the login screen.
- Sessions are protected; signing out on a shared device ends the session there.

<Aside type="caution">
JARAI will never ask for your password by email. Treat any such request as phishing
and contact [support](/general/contact-support/).
</Aside>

## Your data & privacy

You have rights over your personal data (access, correction, erasure). How to
exercise them is covered in [Your privacy rights (GDPR)](/customers/privacy-rights/).

<Aside type="note">
If you administer the account for others, see [Team & seats](/customers/team-and-seats/)
to manage who has access and at what level.
</Aside>
