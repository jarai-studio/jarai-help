JARAI notifies you when something needs your attention — most importantly when a
production is **ready for your review and approval**. This page explains what triggers
a notification and how to tune what you receive.

<VideoHelper title="Stay on top of reviews" duration="60 sec">

1. When a production is ready, you're notified (in-app and, where enabled, by email/push).
2. Open the notification to jump straight to the production awaiting your decision.
3. Review the assets and **Approve** or **Reject**.
4. Set your notification preferences in account settings so you only get what's useful.

</VideoHelper>

## What you get notified about

- **A production is awaiting your approval** — the most important one; nothing publishes without your decision.
- **Status changes** on productions you follow (e.g. published, or a problem that needs attention).
- **Account & billing** notices (e.g. a subscription or payment event).

## Channels

Depending on what's enabled for your account, notifications can reach you:

- **In the Client Portal** — a notification indicator and list.
- **By email** — to the address on your profile.
- **Push** — if you've installed the portal as an app and allowed notifications on your device.

<Aside type="tip">
The portal works as an installable app (PWA). Installing it and allowing
notifications means you'll hear about reviews even when the tab is closed.
</Aside>

## Setting preferences

Manage which notifications you receive, and on which channels, in
[Account settings](/customers/account-settings/). If you're missing notifications,
check there first, then confirm your email address is correct and that device
notifications are allowed.

<Aside type="note">
Approvals are time-sensitive — content waits for your decision. If several people
review on your side, agree who owns approvals so productions don't sit waiting.
</Aside>
