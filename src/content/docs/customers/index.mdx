Welcome. You're using the **JARAI Client Portal** to review productions that an operator has produced for you, approve or reject them, manage your subscription, and exercise your privacy rights.

<VideoHelper title="Customer overview" duration="60 sec">

What you'll do in the Client Portal:

1. **Sign in** from your invite email.
2. **Review productions** an operator built for you — preview every asset.
3. **Approve or reject** — nothing publishes until you approve, including per-platform rights review.
4. **Manage** your subscription, notifications, and privacy rights.

Start with the cards below.

</VideoHelper>
