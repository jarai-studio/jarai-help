Manage your subscription, capacity, and invoices from the **Billing** area of the
Client Portal. JARAI bills on a subscription tier that includes a monthly capacity
allowance, with optional top-ups when you need more.

<VideoHelper src="/videos/customer-billing.mp4" title="Manage your subscription" duration="90 sec">

1. Open **Billing** in the Client Portal.
2. See your current **tier** and **usage** for the month.
3. **Change plan** (upgrade/downgrade) or **buy extra capacity** when you run low.
4. Download **invoices** for your records.

</VideoHelper>

## Your plan

Your tier sets your monthly capacity and features:

| Tier | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Creator** | Individuals getting started |
| **Pro** | Regular producers — more capacity + priority processing |
| **Atelier** | Teams — pooled capacity across seats |

Current pricing is on [jarai.studio](https://www.jarai.studio). Paying **annually**
carries a discount over monthly.

## Capacity & top-ups

Each plan includes a monthly **allowance** that productions draw on. If you need more
than your plan includes in a given month, buy **extra capacity** — it's added to your
balance and used after the monthly allowance.

<Aside type="tip">
Enable **auto-top-up** if you'd rather not be interrupted — capacity is added
automatically when you run low so productions keep flowing.
</Aside>

## Changing your plan

Upgrade or downgrade from the Billing area. Upgrades take effect right away
(more capacity now); downgrades apply from your next billing cycle. Your existing
productions and data are unaffected by a plan change.

## Invoices & payment

Download invoices from the Billing area for your accounting. If a payment fails,
you'll be notified and prompted to update your payment method — productions may pause
until billing is current.

<Aside type="note">
On the **Atelier** team plan, billing is at the team level and capacity pools across
seats — see [Team & seats](/customers/team-and-seats/).
</Aside>
