When an operator invites you to JARAI, you'll receive an email with a sign-in link to the **Client Portal** (`portal.jarai.studio`). Here's how to complete onboarding.

2. **Sign in** (or sign up with Google if your invite uses that address).
3. **Land in the portal** — you'll see the productions made for your account.

## Two ways to get started

You either **sign up yourself** (self-serve) or are **invited by an operator**. The
self-serve path is just below; if you were invited, skip to
[Joining from an invite](#joining-from-an-invite).

## Signing up yourself

<Steps>

1. **Register.** Enter your name and billing country and accept the Terms of Service and
   Privacy Notice. No card is needed to start — you begin on a **free evaluation**.

2. **Verify your email.** We email you a secure sign-in link. It's **single-use** and expires
   after **15 minutes** — click it and you land in your workspace.

3. **Find your plan (optional).** A short quiz asks how many brands/channels you'll produce
   for, whether teammates need their own logins, and whether you need invoicing, a contract or
   SSO. **Creator** and **Pro** are self-serve; **Atelier** and **Enterprise** hand off to
   sales. You can skip the quiz and pick a plan directly.

4. **Choose a plan.** **Creator** is free; **Pro** is a flat fee (about 15% less if you pay
   annually). The exact price is shown before you pay — see
   [Billing and subscriptions](/customers/billing/). Creator starts straight away; Pro
   continues to payment.

5. **Pay (Pro only).** Review the order summary, then complete payment on **Stripe's secure
   checkout**. Cards are handled by Stripe, never stored by JARAI.

6. **Take the product tour** and finish first-run setup — your first account and production
   plan. After that you're in the console, ready to produce.

</Steps>

## Joining from an invite

<Steps>

1. **Click the sign-in link in your invite email.**

   The link is single-use and expires after 7 days. If it expired, ask your operator to resend.

2. **Choose your sign-in method.**

   - **Email + password** — set a password during first login.
   - **Sign in with Google** — link your Google account for passwordless future logins.

   Both options support 2FA when enabled in your account settings.

3. **Land on your account home.**

   You'll see:
   - **Productions** — list of all content JARAI has produced for you
   - **Subscription** — your current plan, usage, and next-renewal date
   - **Settings** — account preferences, notification settings, privacy controls

4. **(Optional) Enable notifications.**

   By default, you'll get an email when a new production is ready to review. Turn this off in Settings if you'd rather check manually.

5. **See your first production.**

   When the operator triggers a production for your account, it'll appear in the **Productions** list. Click it to see preview thumbnails, audio players, and the full Story tab.

</Steps>

## What you control as a customer

- **Approve or reject** every production before it's published anywhere
- **Pause or cancel** your subscription at any time (cancellations honour the rolling-window terms in your contract)
- **Request data export or deletion** under GDPR — see [Your privacy rights](/customers/privacy-rights/)
- **Block specific avatars or themes** from being used in future productions

## What the operator controls

- The **pipeline configuration** — which AI providers, which steps, which platforms to distribute to
- The **content brief** — what topic, what duration, what avatar
- The **subscription tier** — sets your token allowance + per-production limits

You can request changes to any of these by contacting your operator directly (their email is in the account settings) or by [contacting support](/general/contact-support/).
