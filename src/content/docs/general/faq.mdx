## General

### What does JARAI Studio do, exactly?

It's an autonomous content production pipeline. You give it a topic and an avatar; it produces a finished video / podcast / book / image set by chaining ~15 pipeline steps that each call AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, ElevenLabs, etc.). See [What is JARAI Studio?](/getting-started/what-is-jarai/) for the longer answer.

### Is the content JARAI produces unique?

Yes. Every production is generated fresh — JARAI doesn't republish or remix existing content. Two operators triggering the "same" topic will get different outputs (different scene structures, different image picks, different VO performances).

### Is JARAI used for deepfakes?

No. JARAI can clone voices (with explicit speaker-consent attestation) and generate AI imagery, but it does not produce deepfake video impersonations of real people without consent. Our [Acceptable Use Policy](/general/legal/) explicitly prohibits CSAM, NCII, terrorist content, and targeting of specific real children.

## For operators

### How long does a production take end-to-end?

Typically 15–30 minutes for a short-form video, depending on length + how many AI calls are needed. Long-form (15+ min videos, podcast episodes, book chapters) takes 1–3 hours.

### What happens if an AI provider goes down mid-production?

Each pipeline step retries on transient failures (5xx, timeouts). If retries exhaust, the step dead-letters and you'll see a Failed badge with an error code. Most steps have a fallback chain (e.g., voiceover falls through 6 TTS providers). See [Troubleshooting](/operators/troubleshooting/).

### Can I edit the script before voiceover?

Yes — productions pause at editable boundaries if you've enabled review gates. Otherwise, you can retry a step with an override.

## For customers

### Why does JARAI show me warnings about copyright / safety / attribution?

JARAI's analysis flags potential risks for your awareness but doesn't block you. You, as publisher, decide whether to proceed. See [Approval and rights review](/customers/rights-review/) for the full explanation.

### Can I refuse a production?

Yes. Click **Reject** on any production you don't want published — it'll never go to any platform. You can also request a different topic, avatar, or platform mix via your operator.

### What about my data — is JARAI GDPR compliant?

Yes. See [Your privacy rights](/customers/privacy-rights/) for the eight data-subject rights and how to exercise them.

## Billing

### How is usage billed?

Token allowance per month based on your subscription tier. Each AI call (image gen, TTS, video composition, etc.) consumes a budgeted number of tokens. Operators can buy top-ups when allowance runs low.

### What happens at end of billing cycle?

Unused allowance does NOT roll over by default. Top-up packs (one-time purchases) do roll over.

### Can I cancel mid-cycle?

Yes. Cancellation takes effect at the next renewal — you keep service for the remainder of the paid period.
