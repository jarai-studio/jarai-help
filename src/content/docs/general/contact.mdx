Use the form below to reach us about anything — support, a bug, billing, your account, a privacy request, a takedown, or a press enquiry. Pick the closest category and we'll route it to the right place.

<Aside type="tip" title="What happens next">
When you send the form we create a **case** and email you a **reference** (like `JAR-7K3Q9F2M`) plus a secure link to [track its status](/general/track/). You'll get an acknowledgment with the next steps and the timeline for your request. Quote your reference if you contact us again.
</Aside>

## Other ways to reach us

The form above is the fastest route and gives you a tracked case reference. If you'd rather email, these addresses are monitored:

- **General**: [hello@jarai.studio](mailto:hello@jarai.studio)
- **Privacy & data rights**: [privacy@jarai.studio](mailto:privacy@jarai.studio)
- **Legal & takedowns**: [legal@jarai.studio](mailto:legal@jarai.studio)

## Specific routes

- **GDPR data-subject requests** (access, erasure, portability) — pick **Data & privacy request (GDPR)** above, or see [Your privacy rights](/customers/privacy-rights/) for the in-app flows.
- **Takedown / IP / publicity notices** — pick **Copyright / content takedown** above (see [how takedowns work](/general/takedown-notice/)).
- **Abuse, harassment or illegal content** — pick **Abuse / safety report** above; these go to our highest-priority queue.
