JARAI Studio is committed to making its products usable by everyone, including people
who rely on assistive technology.

## The standard we target

We aim to meet **WCAG 2.1 level AA** across the Studio Console, the Client Portal, the
Developer Portal, the marketing site, and this help centre. In practice that means:

- **Keyboard operability** — interactive controls are reachable and usable without a mouse.
- **Accessible names** — buttons, links, and form fields expose a clear name to screen readers (icon-only controls carry an explicit label).
- **Structure** — pages use proper headings, landmarks, and reading order.
- **Reduced motion** — animations are suppressed when your system requests reduced motion.
- **Contrast** — text and essential UI meet AA contrast against the brand palette.
- **Forms** — labels are associated with their controls, and errors are announced.

<Aside type="note">
Accessibility is treated as a build requirement, not an afterthought — automated
checks run in our pipelines and the codebase carries guards against common
regressions (for example, an unlabelled icon button fails a test rather than shipping).
</Aside>

## Media & captions

Generated video and audio can include **captions**, **transcripts**, and **audio
descriptions**. Coverage is tracked on the Accessibility dashboard so gaps are visible
and can be closed.

## Known limitations

No product is perfectly accessible at every moment. Where we're aware of a barrier,
we prioritise fixing it. If you hit something that blocks you, please tell us — that's
the fastest way to get it fixed.

## Report an accessibility barrier

Contact us via [Contact support](/general/contact-support/) and describe:

- the page or feature,
- what you were trying to do,
- the assistive technology and browser/device you were using.

We'll acknowledge your report and work to resolve it.
