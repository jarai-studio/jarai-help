JARAI Studio improves continuously — new capabilities, providers, languages and fixes
ship regularly. This page explains how we release and where to track changes.

## How we ship

- **Continuous delivery** — improvements roll out frequently rather than in big infrequent releases. Most changes are additive and require nothing from you.
- **Backwards-compatible by default** — we avoid breaking existing productions and integrations. Enum values and identifiers are stable once live, so historical productions keep their meaning.
- **Documentation kept in step** — this help centre and the [Developer Portal](https://developer.jarai.studio) are updated alongside the product.

## What tends to change

- **New AI providers & models** — added to the catalogue and wired into the selection chain; existing productions are unaffected unless you opt in.
- **New languages & accents** — the voice catalogue and [accent taxonomy](/administrators/languages-voices/) grow over time.
- **New distribution channels** — added as platform integrations are approved.
- **Quality & reliability** — pipeline and dashboard improvements.

## Staying informed

- **Status & incidents** — operational status and incident history live on the [Status page](/general/status/).
- **API changes** — developers should watch the [Developer Portal](https://developer.jarai.studio); the reference there is always current.
- **In-product** — significant user-facing changes surface in the app where relevant.

<Aside type="note">
A detailed public changelog will be linked here as it's published. In the meantime,
the [Status page](/general/status/) covers operational changes and the Developer

</Aside>

## Questions about a change

If something looks different and you're not sure why, check this help centre first,
then [Contact support](/general/contact-support/).
