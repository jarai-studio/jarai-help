The terms you'll meet across the Studio Console, Client Portal, and this help centre.

## Content & production

- **Production** — one full run of the pipeline for a single brief, including every generated asset and the final deliverables.
- **Brief** — a production request: topic/subject, project type, avatar(s), and options. The pipeline starts from the brief.
- **Project type** — the kind of output (short-form video, long-form video, podcast, article, book…). It sets which pipeline steps run.
- **Content theme** — the subject space the brief generator works within (e.g. "tech news", "recipes").
- **Pipeline step** — one of the ~15 automated stages a production moves through (brief → narrative → script → images → voice → video → variants → packaging). See [Production pipeline](/operators/pipeline/).
- **Deliverable** — a final, packaged per-platform asset (e.g. the 9:16 TikTok video, the 16:9 LinkedIn video, the podcast MP3).
- **Asset** — an individual piece produced along the way (a sourced/generated image, a voiceover clip, captions).
- **Attribution package** — the aggregated sources, licences and credits for everything used in a production.

## Avatars & voices

- **Avatar** — a persona used in productions: a **likeness** (on-screen face) plus a **voice variant**.
- **Voice variant** — one specific TTS voice (provider + accent + character), e.g. *ElevenLabs — en-GB Yorkshire — calm*.
- **Accent (taxonomy)** — a precise regional label for a voice, from country level (e.g. `es-ES`) down to sub-regional (e.g. `es-ES-x-andalucia-sevilla`).
- **Trait facet** — a filterable voice attribute: accent, age, gender, style, use-case, tags, colour.

## People & roles

- **Operator** — a Studio Console user who produces content (`AccountOperator`).
- **Customer** — a Client Portal user who reviews and approves content (`CustomerAdministrator` / `CustomerViewer`).
- **Platform administrator** — manages shared platform infrastructure across all accounts (`PlatformAdministrator`).
- **Account** — the workspace a set of productions and avatars belongs to.
- **Customer (entity)** — the organisation a set of accounts belongs to; billing rolls up here.

## Pipeline & operations

- **Loop** — a stage of the wider system: Loop 1 produces content, Loop 2 distributes it, later loops handle engagement, optimisation and promotion.
- **Distribution** — publishing approved content to your connected channels (Loop 2).
- **Gate** — a check a production must pass before it can advance (e.g. the final gate before distribution).
- **Quality score** — a per-step measure of output quality, compared against your account's quality floor.
- **Dead-letter queue (DLQ)** — where a permanently-failed pipeline message is parked for an operator to inspect and resubmit.
- **Idempotency** — the property that re-running a step that already completed has no harmful effect (so retries are safe).

## Billing & access

- **Token allowance** — the budgeted AI-call units included with your subscription each month.
- **Top-up / extra capacity** — additional allowance you can buy when you run low.
- **Tier** — your subscription level (Creator / Pro / Atelier).
- **RLS (row-level security)** — the database mechanism that isolates each account's data so one account can never read another's.
