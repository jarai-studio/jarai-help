This page is being built. A brief subset:

- **Avatar** — A character profile (face + voice variant) used to narrate productions.
- **Brief** — A specific production request, with a topic, avatar, duration, and target platforms.
- **Voice variant** — One specific TTS voice (provider + accent + characteristic combination). E.g., "ElevenLabs Rachel — en-US-x-newyork — calm".
- **Pipeline step** — One of the ~15 automated stages a brief moves through (narrative → script → image → voice → video → distribution).
- **Production** — A completed run-through of the pipeline for a single brief. Includes all generated assets.
- **Deliverable** — A final per-platform asset (e.g., the 9:16 TikTok video, the 16:9 LinkedIn video, the podcast MP3).
- **Token allowance** — Budgeted AI-call units per month, tied to your subscription tier.
- **Operator** — User of the Studio Console; produces content.
- **Customer** — User of the Client Portal; reviews and approves content.

More definitions coming. For specific terms not listed here, see [FAQ](/general/faq/) or [Contact support](/general/contact-support/).
