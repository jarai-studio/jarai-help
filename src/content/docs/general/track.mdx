When you contact us we email you an acknowledgement with a **secure tracking link** and a reference (like `JAR-7K3Q9F2M`). Open that link to see the current status of your request here.

<TrackCase />

<Aside type="note" title="Why do I need the link from my email?">
The tracking link is signed and time-limited, so only you (and anyone you forward the email to) can see your request's status — we don't ask you to create an account or a password. If your link has expired, just email us your reference and we'll update you.
</Aside>

## Need to reach us again?

- **Start a new request**: [Contact us](/general/contact/)

Always quote your reference (`JAR-…`) so we can find your case quickly.
