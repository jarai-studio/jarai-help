JARAI Studio Console is also a **mobile app** — a progressive web app (PWA) you can install on
your phone for quick, on-the-go access to alerts, approvals, budgets and platform controls. The
mobile experience lives under `/m` and adapts to your role.

<VideoHelper src="mobile-app.mp4" title="Install and use the mobile app">

1. Open the console on your phone and tap **Install** when the banner appears.
2. Allow **notifications** so you're alerted when something needs you.
3. Use the bottom tabs — alerts, productions/actions, budgets — sized for one hand.
4. It keeps working **offline**, syncing your actions when you reconnect.

</VideoHelper>

## Install to your home screen

When you open the console on a supported mobile browser, a banner offers **"Install JARAI for
quick access."**

<Steps>

1. **Tap Install** — your browser shows its native add-to-home-screen prompt; confirm it.

2. **Or tap Later** to dismiss for now (it'll offer again next session).

3. **Launch from your home screen** — it opens full-screen like a native app. On iOS, use
   **Share → Add to Home Screen** if no banner appears.

</Steps>

## Push notifications

Turn on notifications so alerts reach you without opening the app.

<Steps>

1. **Allow notifications** when prompted (or from **Notifications** in the app).

2. **Choose which severities** you want — Critical, High, Warning, Info. The default sends
   **Critical** and **High** only, so you're not buzzed for noise.

3. **If they're blocked**, the app tells you — re-enable notifications for the site in your
   browser/OS settings.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Notification preferences are per device. Set them on each phone you use, and adjust severities
any time from the **Notifications** screen.
</Aside>

## What you can do on mobile

The bottom tabs follow your role:

| Role | Tabs |
|---|---|
| **Platform administrator** | **Alerts**, **System health** (loops + provider health + KPIs), **Loop controls** (platform mode + loop/feature switches), **Budget** (platform costs) |
| **Operator / Customer admin** | **Alerts**, **Productions**, **Actions** (start a production, work the approvals queue), **Budget** (account P&L) |
| **Customer viewer / Finance** | **Account P&L**, **Portfolio**, **Distribution** |

Handy gestures: **swipe** an alert to acknowledge or resolve it, and **pull down** to refresh.
Destructive platform controls (like disabling a loop) ask you to re-confirm — and, where your
device supports it, re-authenticate.

## Working offline

The app keeps working when you lose signal:

- A banner shows **"Offline — showing cached data"** so you know you're looking at the last
  synced view.
- **Write actions you take are queued** and replay automatically when you reconnect (on browsers
  that support background sync, or the next time the app is online).
- Some actions are **paused** while offline rather than queued — you'll see *"You're offline —
  changes are paused until you reconnect."* Retry once you're back online.

<Aside type="tip">
The mobile app mirrors the desktop console — **Loop controls** maps to
[platform controls](/administrators/platform-controls/), **Alerts** to the alerts feed, and the
**P&L / Distribution** tabs to the matching [dashboards](/operators/dashboards/). Use the desktop
console for deep configuration; use mobile to stay on top of alerts and approvals.
</Aside>
