## Choice of law

JARAI Studio is governed by the laws of **England & Wales**. Disputes are resolved in English courts unless your local consumer-protection law gives you the right to bring claims in your own jurisdiction (which, in the EU, you usually do).

## Key clauses (summary, not legal advice)

- **Rights warranty by user**: When you publish content via JARAI, you warrant you have the rights to do so.
- **Indemnification**: You indemnify JARAI against third-party claims arising from your published content.
- **Limitation of liability**: Capped at fees paid in the last 12 months or £100, whichever is higher.
- **Termination**: Either party can terminate the subscription with notice per your specific plan.
