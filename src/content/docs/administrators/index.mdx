Platform administrators hold the **`PlatformAdministrator`** role. You manage the
shared infrastructure every account depends on — the AI providers and models, the
language and voice catalogue, platform connectors, pricing and P&L, and the
compliance and operations tooling. Most of these surfaces live under **Settings**
and **Dashboards** in the Studio Console and are visible only to administrators.

<Aside type="caution" title="Admin-only, platform-wide">
Changes here affect **every** account, not just your own. Enum ordinals, provider
configuration, and pricing are deliberately hard to change once live. Read the
relevant page before editing.
</Aside>

<VideoHelper title="Platform admin console tour" duration="2 min">

A tour of the administrator surfaces (each is documented in its own page):

1. **AI providers & models** — register providers, models, roles and capabilities; watch provider health.
2. **Languages & voices** — the language catalogue, voice variants and the accent taxonomy.
3. **Platforms & distribution** — distribution connectors and platform controls.
4. **Finance & P&L** — account / customer / platform profit-and-loss, markup and budgets.
5. **Compliance & governance** — DSAR, takedowns, the operator audit log, content safety.
6. **Operations & reliability** — alerts, escalation, the dead-letter queue and production recovery.

## Roles at a glance

| Role | Sees | Typical use |
|---|---|---|
| `PlatformAdministrator` | Everything, all accounts | You — platform operations |
| `AccountOperator` | Their account's productions/media | Day-to-day content production |
| `CustomerAdministrator` | Their customer's accounts (Client Portal) | Customer-side review & billing |
| `CustomerViewer` | Read-only customer view | Stakeholder visibility |
| `FinanceViewer` | P&L dashboards only | Finance reporting |

<Aside type="note">
Administrators are also operators — you can produce content in any account. The
admin surfaces below are the *additional* responsibilities the role unlocks.
</Aside>
