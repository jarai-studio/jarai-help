JARAI Studio is an **autonomous content production pipeline** that uses generative AI to produce video, audio, image, and text content end-to-end. It's built for organisations that want to produce branded content at scale without managing the AI provider stack themselves.

<VideoHelper src="/videos/overview.mp4" title="What is JARAI Studio?" duration="90 sec">

JARAI turns a short brief into finished, ready-to-publish media — automatically.

- **You describe what you want** — a project type (video, podcast, article…) and a theme.
- **A 15-step AI pipeline runs** — script, images, voiceover, video, packaging.
- **You review and approve** — operators in the Console, customers in the Client Portal.
- **JARAI distributes and tracks** — approved content publishes to your channels.

The rest of this page covers what it produces and who uses it.

## What it produces

- **Short-form video** for social platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn)
- **Long-form video** for podcast-style episodes and educational content
- **Audio-only** for podcast feeds + audiobook chapters
- **Image stills** for stock-marketplace listings and editorial use
- **Article + book manuscripts** with derivative formats (audiobook, e-pub, print)

Every production runs through a multi-step pipeline (Steps 1-15) covering brief generation → narrative → scene scripting → image acquisition → voiceover → soundtrack → video composition → platform-variant rendering → SEO metadata → attribution → quality gates → distribution.

## The two surfaces

| Surface | URL | Audience | What they do |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Studio Console** | `studio.jarai.studio` | Operators | Configure pipelines, manage avatars, monitor productions, distribute deliverables |
| **Client Portal** | `portal.jarai.studio` | Customers | Review productions, approve or reject content, manage subscriptions, exercise privacy rights |

## How content gets produced

1. **Operator triggers** a production from the Studio Console (manually, on a schedule, or from a content brief).
2. **JARAI's pipeline** runs Steps 1-15 autonomously, calling AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, ElevenLabs, etc.) per step.
3. **Customer reviews** the resulting production in the Client Portal — approves it (publishes), rejects it (deletes), or requests changes.
4. **Distribution** ships the approved content to the customer's chosen platforms (Loop 2 of the pipeline).
5. **Performance summarisation** (Loop 3-4) tracks engagement and feeds it back into future productions.
