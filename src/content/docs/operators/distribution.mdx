Once a production is finished and you **approve it to publish**, JARAI distributes it to
your connected channels and tracks the results. This page covers that flow end to end.

<VideoHelper title="Publish to your channels" duration="2 min">

1. **Connect your channels** once via OAuth (Settings → Connected platforms).
2. Finish a production and open its detail page.
3. **Approve to publish** — optionally deselect platforms you don't want this time.
4. JARAI posts to each in-scope channel and records the published URLs.
5. Track publications + engagement on the **Distribution** dashboard.

</VideoHelper>

## Before you can publish

You need at least one **connected channel**. Connecting is a one-time OAuth step per
platform — see [Connected platforms](/operators/connected-platforms/). After that,
publishing is automatic on approval.

## Approving to publish

<Steps>

1. **Open the finished production** (it reaches an "awaiting publish" state once the deliverables are packaged).

2. **Review the deliverables** — preview each platform variant on the Story tab.

3. **Choose platforms.** By default JARAI publishes to the channels in scope; you can **deselect** any platform you don't want for this production. Deselected platforms are suppressed (not published); the rest go ahead.

4. **Approve.** JARAI hands the production to the distribution loop, which posts the right variant to each selected channel.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
Per-platform **rights review** happens here too — acknowledge any advisory warnings
(copyright/likeness/attribution) before approving. See
[Approval and rights review](/customers/rights-review/) for the customer-side view of
the same gate.
</Aside>

## What happens after approval

- JARAI posts the **platform-specific variant** (correct aspect ratio, duration, and SEO metadata) to each connected channel.
- It records the **published URL** for each.
- **Engagement and revenue** flow back in and appear on the **Distribution** dashboard.

## Tracking results

Use **Dashboards → Distribution** to see what published where, the live URLs, and the
engagement/revenue events tied to each. The **Avatar performance** dashboard breaks
results down by avatar and platform.

## When a publish doesn't go through

- **Channel disconnected / token expired** → reconnect it (Settings → Connected platforms). JARAI refreshes tokens automatically where it can, but a revoked authorisation needs re-consent.
- **Platform still pending approval** → some platforms require app review before production posting; content for those is held rather than failing.
- **Nothing in scope** → make sure at least one connected channel was selected at approval.

<Aside type="note">
Distribution runs after the production pipeline completes — it's a separate stage, so
a production can be fully produced and waiting on your approval before anything posts.
Nothing publishes without that approval.
</Aside>
