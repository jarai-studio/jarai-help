The **Media** section is where every asset a production generates lives — images,
audio, video, transcripts, and the final packaged deliverables. Use it to preview
results, download files, and trace an asset back to the production that made it.

<VideoHelper src="media-library.mp4" title="Find and download your media" duration="90 sec">

1. Open **Media** from the left sidebar.
2. Browse assets (videos show a hover/scrub thumbnail) or filter to a production.
3. Click an asset to open its **detail drawer** — type, the production it belongs to, and a preview.
4. **Download** — files save as `JARAI - <Production name>.<ext>`, not a raw UUID.

</VideoHelper>

## Assets vs deliverables

- **Assets** are the individual pieces produced along the way — sourced/generated images, voiceover audio, the assembled video, captions, transcripts.
- **Deliverables** are the final, packaged outputs (with platform variants and the attribution package) produced at the end of the pipeline. The **Master deliverable registry** lists these.

## Previewing

- **Images** show inline.
- **Audio** plays in an inline player.
- **Video** uses a lightweight poster/scrub thumbnail in the grid and a full player in the detail drawer — so the list stays fast.

## Downloading

Downloads use a friendly, human filename — **`JARAI - <Production name>.<ext>`** —
derived from the production name and the file's real extension, so you never get an
opaque blob UUID on disk. You can download a single asset or grab everything for a
production at once.

<Aside type="tip">
The asset detail drawer links back to its **production** — handy when you've found
an asset in the library and want to see how (and why) it was made.
</Aside>

<Aside type="note">
Old assets are tiered to cheaper storage over time by a background job. They remain
downloadable; the first fetch of a very old asset may take a moment longer.
</Aside>
