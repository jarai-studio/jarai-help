JARAI Studio turns a short brief into finished, ready-to-publish media — automatically.

1. **You describe what you want** — a project type (short-form video, podcast, article, book…) and a theme. Operators do this in the **Studio Console**; partners can do it via the **API**.
2. **A 15-step AI pipeline runs** — it writes the brief and script, sources and generates images, produces voiceover and video, then packages platform-ready deliverables.
3. **You review and approve** — operators check the result in the Console; customers approve (or reject) in the **Client Portal**, including per-platform rights review.
4. **JARAI distributes and tracks** — approved content publishes to your connected channels, and engagement and revenue flow back in.

Pick your path below depending on whether you produce content (operator), receive it (customer), administer the platform, or build on the API.
